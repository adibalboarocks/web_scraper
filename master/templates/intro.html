Welcome to the Web Scraper - Python Tool

This tool is designed for scraping information from four dedicated websites that provide insights into the telecom industry.

With this tool, you can perform web scraping in two ways:

  1. By specifying a date range, you can scrape news articles published within that period.
  2. Alternatively, you can scrape articles directly from the first page of the news section, including their summaries and links.

The tool will scrape the headlines and paragraphs from the web pages, consolidate the data, and present the results on a separate page.
